About Us

AboutCCS LR

In the effort to upgrade the research capabilities of the College of Computer Studies, Engr. Marc Angelo P. Martinez with the approval the CCS College Dean, Dr. Albert A. Vinluan, selected students from the third-year and second-year level. Each student was trained in different skills, specifically for topic construction and technical writing, and assessed concerning work discipline, responsibility, and cooperation. Today, the League continues to expand its activities by hosting a seminar on the 26th of February, 2019. The League hopes that this activity will help achieve its purpose and provide a service to the academe.

Purpose

The CCS League of Researchers aims to provide training for research making and produce quality research papers for the interest of the College of Computer Studies, the University, and above all, for the glory of our Almighty God.

The seminar aims to teach the learning, failing, and success experiences as a staple in Research and Development to the participating audience. It is intended for the audience to think and be part of the technological advancement of society using research.

Professional experience in building and developing new technological achievements through a research-based foundation will be shared in the seminar, tackling topics across the fields of Machine Learning, Entrepreneurship, Data Science, and Internet of Things. Most importantly, the value of failure is emphasized as it is a stepping stone to learning and, ultimately, to success.
