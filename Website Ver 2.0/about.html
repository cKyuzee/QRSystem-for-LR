Project Description

This website serves to show the public a general overview of a forecasted yield for rice grain crops in Nueva Ecija. The forecast shown is calculated by a neural network trained specifically for this purpose. The data used and displayed here are all acquired with permission from the Department of Agriculture, the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration, and Freedom of Information. The variables used for the forecast were determined through careful review of previous researches, both foreign and local. Accuracy of the neural network forecast has been validated based on comparisons of forecasts done for the previous years against their actual yields. With this easily accesible website, we hope to give all farmers and related bodies in Nueva Ecija necessary insights for developing crop trends. And finally, the project aims to spread its coverage to the entire nation.
